Vertus Perfume    

Packaging box design  

Assignment: Two start up companies Vertus and Aljass are preparing to launch their companies in Singapore. We were tasked to design an appealing design package for one of the companies. I have choosen to design for Vertus, a luxury brand perfume which produces scents such as citrus and orientia, both for men and women and is famous worldwide.


Portfolio

My creative process 

1. Brainstorming and ideating  



I have done some research on what are the latest perfume packaging box trends and asked my friends and classmates on what they think is a good perfume packaging box. I planned out different rough sketches of how I think the perfume box should look like, so that I have more choices to choose from.  The three ideas were :

1. paper-cutting pattern design made up of flowers, lemon, and wood

2.Paper cutting images of citrus fruits and leaves which gives off a masculine appeal since this is a unisex perfume.

2.Box Prototype 


I have decided to test my perfume packaging box out to find out if the dimensions are correct and suit the size of the packaging box before I begin designing my final product to ensure that the process is going through smoothly. I have also received feedback from users saying that this box is better as at once glance you would be able to see the box.

3. Artwork Pattern Designs

Next, I did some artwork pattern designs to give users a gist of which patterns to they think would be more suitable to place on the box so that the choice is better. They mentioned that the second box is a better option because the placement of flowers can be manipulated to show the direction and looks more interesting and appealing and gives off a more fresh and vibrant look.


4.Final product

All done! After reviewing feedbacks , I decided to go with the above flora and citrus packaging designs. Do check out above GIF for the 3d mockup version of the packaging designs.
