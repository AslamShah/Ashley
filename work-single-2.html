You Can Change the World:Kids guide to better planet 

Kids Catalogue 

Assignment: We were given a task to create a catalogue design based on our project assignment brief. I decided to work on a kids catalogue, to educate them on recycling with fun graphics and text that they can relate to.        

Portfolio

My creative process 

1. Brainstorming and ideating  


I have came up with a few sketches of typography font and a few magazine layout sketches. I wanted the font type to look more fun and playful. And I wanted to create simple and organised layout for users  to easily navigate through the pages.

2.Typography and catalogue samples

I then further expanded my ideas and made them into prototypes. I created a set of typography fonts inspired by a kidâ€™s handwriting, with some alphabets shaped like toys like for example a spade, to give it an interesting look and feel and to attract this younger target audience. I also came up with a few layout sketches for my catalogue design. Color scheme was changed to be more contrasting and I simplified the layouts, as I received feedback that my previous layouts were too complicated.


3. Test

I am done! I have came up with a final mockup of my kids catalogue. By using hand-drawn kids-like artwork images and simple illustrations, I hope that this catalogue can appeal to children. The purpose of this catalogue is also to educate children on recycling. Children will also be induced to read the catalogue through the fun and cute illustrations using hierarchy, proximity, and repetition of simple shapes. Coloured texts on every paged help to ensure consistency and emphasis as well.
