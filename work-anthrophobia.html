TITLE
Hear Me Out
LOGLINE
A girl with anthropophobia finds it difficult to understand herself. However, through many of her real-life encounters and setbacks, she manages to see and enjoy the happier things in life.
SUMMARY
Hear me out" is a therapeutic story that can be helpful for people affected by similar phobia. The story centers on the major and only character Amelia. Amelia is a young beautiful lady in her mid-twenties, she is an introvert that suffers from anxiety and the anthropobia. Amelia spends all her days indoors, reading and writing articles. She wishes to reach out to the world through her articles but couldn’t due to her phobia to go out and meet people or been looked at. She tries to fight that phobia after a harsh word from her parents but to no avail. She finds a way to actualize her dream, which is for her to reach out to the world through her articles, through a freelance site.
PLOT DEVELOPMENT
Act one: Amelia, an independent young lady in her early 2o’s. Amelia in her circle of life, fights her clinical disorder. She suffers from anthropophobia, limiting her from exploring things she ever wishes to. She spends her days all alone indoors, reading and writing. Amelia is a wordsmith who wishes to touch lives with her articles. Amelia experience’s what real life is through her television, watching news, wishing she could touch people’s life through her articles. 
Act two: Amelia’s life begins to turn around after she receive an urgent call from her parents. Upon hearing harsh words and advice from her parents, she decided to confront her challenges. She attempts going out, Awkward conversations at dinner date, losing her phone that she hid at the cabinet, hiding in the toilet, crying, wearing earphones while covering her face at the subway station. She finds it difficult to converse with others and days go by but she doesn't know how to deal with it.
  Act three: Amelia opens her laptop and brows on how to go out and convers with people, then she bombs into a freelance site that says “Let your voice reach the world through writing and get paid” She quickly register on the site, set up her profile and post one of her articles she has written down in her dairy. Hours later, she started having impressions and comments on her article. People with nice comments on how they love her articles and urges that they await her articles.
Resolution: Under few months, Amelia started having subscribers and started making good money from her articles. She then wakes up every morning feeling fulfilled that her greatest desire has come through.
CHARACTER ANALYSIS.
Amelia: A young girl and the only child of her parents. Amelia has a clinical disorder ‘anthropophobia”, fear of people and crowed. This turns her into been an introvert. Amelia has dreams and desires but it’s been limited due to the disorder. She still finds way to make her dreams come to pass and be happy with her life.
