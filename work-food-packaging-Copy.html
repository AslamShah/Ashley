Vertus Perfume    

Packaging box design  

Assignment: Two start up companies Vertus and Aljass are preparing to launch their companies in Singapore. We were tasked to design an appealing design package for one of the companies. I have chosen to design for Vertus, a luxury unisex brand perfume which produces scents such as citrus and oriential.The focus of the magazine would be for young adults aged 20 to 25 years old who loves luxury brand perfumes and are looking for a unique and special perfume design to attract him or her.      


Portfolio

My creative process 

1. Brainstorming and ideating  



One of my initial idea (as above Box B ) was to create a cigarette box inspired perfume box for a more masculine feel, with the use of  paper-cutting pattern design of flowers, lemon, and wood at some portion of the box. However, after considering, I decided to go with a pull out perfume box (as above A ) instead, with an insert to place the perfume. 

2.Box Prototype 


Next, I tried doing up a mock up of the box to check with dimensions, functionality as well as plan where the graphics and text should be placed at.

 3. Artwork Pattern Designs


Then, I tried out 2 different pattern designs and feedback received were that second flora print design,   is a better option as the flowers can be manipulated to create direction and colors are more appealing and gives off a fresh and vibrant look. 

3. Final product  

All done! After consideration and making several adjustments I have decided to go with the above flora and citrus designs.
