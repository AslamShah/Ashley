Vegan Bakery   

Magazine cover, masthead, logo and packaging design            

Assignment: We were given a task to design a magazine cover, masthead for the magazine, logo design and a festive cake packaging for Vegan Bakery. I started with  some pencil sketches  of my ideas with focus on the target audience aged 25 to 30 years old who are interested in Vegan food products.
